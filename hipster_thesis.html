Hipsters

by Harsh Patel

Overview

Hipster refers to a subculture of young, urban middle class adults and older teenagers that appeared in the
1990s. The subculture us associated with independent music, a varied non-mainstream fashion sensibility,
progressive[1][2] or indepepdent political views, alternative spirituality or atheism/agnosticism, and
alternative lifestyles. Interests in media include independent film, magazines such as Clash, and websites
like Pitchfork Media.
